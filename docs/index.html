AVC One Thing

Here's something worth knowing: **Your brain physically rewires itself based on what you repeatedly do.** Neuroplasticity isn't just a buzzword—it's measurable. London taxi drivers who navigate the city's complex streets for years develop a notably larger hippocampus (the brain region handling spatial memory) compared to bus drivers who follow fixed routes. The practical takeaway: the "I'm just not a math person" or "I can't learn languages" mindset is largely a story we tell ourselves. Struggle and repetition aren't signs you lack talent—they're literally the mechanism by which your brain builds new capacity. What you find hard today can become easier through the unglamorous work of just... doing it repeatedly.
